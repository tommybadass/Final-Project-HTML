StyleWriter II

The StyleWriter II replaced the original. This model, based on a Canon engine that printed at 360 DPI,[2] had twice the memory of its predecessor and double the printing speed. The enclosure was restyled in Apple's "neoclassical" design language of the time. The codename for the model was "Speedracer".
